How should sections animate in as you scroll?

This applies to cards, section headings, and content blocks as they enter the viewport

A

Fade + Slide Up

Elements fade in while rising ~30px from below. Staggered so cards appear one after another. The most universally loved animation — smooth, professional, never distracting.

B

Scale + Fade

Elements scale from 95% → 100% while fading in. Feels more premium and subtle than slide — no movement, just "appearing." Works especially well for cards and hero elements.

C

Mix — Slide Up for cards, Scale for hero

Hero and section headings use scale+fade (feels grand), while grid cards use staggered fade+slide. Different motion for different elements adds life without being overdone.

We'll use Intersection Observer (no extra library) to trigger these only when elements enter the viewport.
